How the race works
1
Runs 1 Apr – 30 Nov 2026. Open to enrolled Founding Partners (max 25). Enrolment can pause when full.
2
Your score is your active referred listings. Each published, active listing from a host who joined via your race link counts one point. Churn drops the point.
3
Commission is a revenue ladder, 10 → 25% of your race book's monthly subscription revenue. It re-rates monthly, applies to the whole book, and keeps paying for life — even after the race ends.
4
Prizes are explicit and audited. Cash prizes plus permanent rate floors, awarded at the published milestones below. The leaderboard freezes at race end.
