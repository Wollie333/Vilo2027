Welcome in

It's less a hotel,
more a home with
spare rooms.

We're a five-room guesthouse in the old parsonage on Church Street — pressed ceilings, deep baths, a long table, and a garden the kitchen raids every morning.

There's no front desk, no piped music, and no fee for booking straight with us. Just a key, a cup of tea on arrival, and whichever room suits you best.
