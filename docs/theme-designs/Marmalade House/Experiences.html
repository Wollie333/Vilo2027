Home·Things to do

Slow days, full ones

Do as little or as much as you like. Most of it begins at the breakfast table and ends on the stoep — but the Karoo's right there when you want it.

close to home

What there is to do

☕

Breakfast & the garden

Long, lazy breakfasts under the vine, then a wander through the fig and herb beds with a second coffee.

⛰

The Swartberg Pass

One of the world's great mountain drives, an hour from the door. We'll pack you a picnic and a flask.

★

Stargazing

Some of the darkest skies in the country. Pieter sets up the telescope on the stoep on clear nights.

✎

The village

Galleries, a bookshop and the best dinner in town — all a two-minute amble from the front door.

✶

Olive & wine tastings

A handful of small farms nearby pour beautifully. We'll book you in and point you the right way.

♨

Just resting

A book, the hammock, the cat. Nobody will mind if you never leave the garden.
