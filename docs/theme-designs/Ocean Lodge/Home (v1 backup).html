Welcome to Oceans View

Barefoot luxury,
steps from the surf

Set right on the white sand of Camps Bay, with Table Mountain at its back and the Atlantic at its feet, Oceans View is a sun-drenched escape built for slow mornings and long, golden evenings.

Forty-eight light-filled rooms and suites, three pools that spill toward the sea, a spa, and two restaurants where the catch arrives daily. Everything you need, and nothing you don't.

The resort

Days you won't want to end

Swim, sail, spa, repeat. Everything at Oceans View is built around one idea — that the best holidays are the ones where you do exactly as little, or as much, as you like.

Pools & beach club

Three pools cascading to the sand, daybeds, and a beach club that runs from breakfast to sundowners.

The spa

Five treatment rooms, a sea-facing sauna, and therapists who know exactly how to undo a long year.

Sea-to-table dining

Two restaurants and a sunset bar. The catch lands in the morning; the Cape's best wines pour all night.
