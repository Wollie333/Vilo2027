Home·Experiences

More than
a bed.

A café the street queues for, a roof you'll struggle to leave, a studio that still makes things — and a city worth the walk. Here's what to do without going far.

In the building

Downstairs & up

The café

Single-origin coffee, all-day plates and the best people-watching on Loop Street — your breakfast, every morning.

The rooftop

Loungers, a small bar and a sunset over Table Mountain. Open to guests from check-in to last light.

Studio workshops

Print, ceramics and the odd late-night exhibition in the working studio off the lobby. Guests welcome.

The record library

Borrow from the lobby crate, play it in your room. Requests taken; snobbery, gently, encouraged.

Sundowner sessions

A guest DJ and a short cocktail list on the roof, Thursday to Saturday, as the light goes.

The little black book

Tables, tickets, a car at dawn — the front desk runs on favours. Ask for the thing you can't get.

Five minutes from the door

The neighbourhood

The walk

Galleries, a bookshop and a bakery you can reach before your room is even ready.

After dark

The wine bars and tiny kitchens we'd actually send a friend to, all within a short stroll.

Sunset spots

The roof, obviously — plus four more we'll mark on your map at check-in.
