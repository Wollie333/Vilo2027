Our story

It started with
a spare floor.

Kira and Sol ran a small design studio on Loop Street, and a spare floor above it they couldn't quite figure out what to do with. So in 2018 they put a bed in it, then another, then twenty more.

Atelier is what happened next — a hotel designed the way we'd design anything else: by hand, in colour, with strong opinions and no committee. We still make the prints on your walls and the ceramics by your sink, downstairs, most afternoons.

It's still just us. Twenty-two rooms, a café, a roof, and a studio that never quite stopped being a studio.

How we do it

Three rules we
actually keep.

Make, don't buy

The art, the ceramics, half the furniture — made by us, or by makers we know by name.

Stay small

Twenty-two rooms is exactly enough to know every guest's name and still sleep at night.

Be straight

Book direct, pay what you see. No agents, no resort fees, no nonsense at checkout.
