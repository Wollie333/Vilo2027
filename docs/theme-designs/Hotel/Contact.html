Frequently asked

Check-in & check-out?

Check-in from 2pm, check-out by 11am. Early or late? Tell us — we'll mind your bags and the café's always open to you.

Is parking included?

Secure parking and fast Wi-Fi are free for every guest. We'll happily arrange an airport car too — just send your flight.

What's included?

Café breakfast, the rooftop, Wi-Fi and parking. Treatments and dinner are extra — and there's never a booking fee or surcharge.

Cancellation policy?

Free cancellation up to 48 hours before arrival on every direct booking, with a full refund. No agency fees on top, ever.
