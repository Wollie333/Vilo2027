Founding Race — Competition Rules

Version 3 · Effective [DATE ON PUBLICATION]
These rules govern the Wielo Founding Race and form part of the Wielo Affiliate Agreement. By entering, you agree to be bound by them.

1. Promoter

The promoter is Mana Marketing (Pty) Ltd ("Wielo", "we", "us"), Mpumalanga, South Africa. These rules are available free of charge at this URL for the duration of the competition and for the period required by the Consumer Protection Act 68 of 2008.

2. Who may enter

Entry is open to Wielo affiliate partners who hold an active partner account and have accepted the Wielo Affiliate Agreement. Entry is limited to 25 partners, allocated in the order partners join. Employees and directors of the promoter, and their immediate families, may not win a cash prize.

3. Entry is free

No purchase or payment of any kind is required to enter or to win. Becoming a Wielo partner is free.

4. Competition period

The competition runs from 21 April 2026 to 21 December 2026 at 23:59 SAST. Only referrals that activate (that is, the referred host starts a paid Wielo subscription) within this period count toward the leaderboard.

5. How scoring works

Your score is the total number of hosts you referred who activated a paid subscription during the competition period. The leaderboard is published and updated regularly. If two or more partners finish on the same score, the partner who reached that score first places higher.

6. Prizes

Cash prizes, and permanent commission floors that continue after the competition ends:

  • 1st place — R15 000 cash, and a permanent commission floor of 20%
  • 2nd place — R7 000 cash, and a permanent commission floor of 15%
  • 3rd place — R3 000 cash, and a permanent commission floor of 12%

Milestone prizes, each awarded once:

  • R2 000 to the first partner to reach 10 activated referrals
  • R1 000 to any partner reaching 5 activated referrals within 30 days
  • R1 000 each month to the partner with the largest net increase that month

A "commission floor" means your commission rate will never fall below that percentage for as long as you remain an active partner in good standing, even if your monthly referred revenue would otherwise place you on a lower rung.

7. Commission (outside the competition)

Independently of any prize, partners earn recurring commission on the subscription revenue of hosts they refer, for as long as those hosts remain subscribed:

  • 10% on monthly referred subscription revenue up to R10 000
  • 15% up to R25 000
  • 20% up to R50 000
  • 25% above R50 000

A once-off conversion bonus of R250 (monthly plan) or R400 (annual plan) is payable per activated referral. Commission is paid in South African Rand and is subject to the payout terms in the Affiliate Agreement, including any clawback where a referred host refunds or charges back.

8. Notification and payment of prizes

Winners will be notified by email at the address on their partner account within 5 business days of the competition closing, and prizes paid to their nominated payout method within 30 days of the winner confirming their details. If a winner cannot be contacted after reasonable attempts within 30 days, the promoter may award the prize to the next-placed partner.

9. Disqualification

The promoter may disqualify a partner and withhold any prize where, acting reasonably, it finds: self-referral or referral of accounts the partner controls; referrals created by fraud, misrepresentation or automated means; sign-ups that are refunded, reversed or cancelled shortly after activating; or breach of the Affiliate Agreement. Disqualification decisions will be explained in writing.

10. Changes and cancellation

The promoter may amend these rules or end the competition early where reasonably necessary, including for legal or technical reasons. Any change will be published at this URL, and partners will be notified. Changes will not retrospectively reduce a prize or a commission floor already earned.

11. Personal information

Personal information is processed in line with the Wielo Privacy Policy and the Protection of Personal Information Act 4 of 2013. Winners' first names and the town or region of their community may be published on the public leaderboard; full contact details will not be.

12. Disputes

Any dispute should first be raised with Wielo support. These rules are governed by South African law.
