The framework

Most content calendars are built around topics or keywords. This one is built around what the customer is feeling at each moment of their journey — and what content will genuinely cut through versus get scrolled past.

2

Tracks

Two audiences, two parallel journeys. Guests (B2C — travellers) and Owners (B2B — lodge hosts). Different pains, different desires, different platforms.

3×3

Stages

Awareness → Consideration → Conversion. Each broken into three sub-stages with defined mental state, desire, and pain point.

30

Concepts

Weighted toward awareness (12 of 30) because awareness is your biggest current friction. Each concept addresses one specific psychological moment.

150

Deliverables

Each of the 30 concepts is rendered for Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, YouTube and LinkedIn in their native sizes. Five versions per concept, on average.

How to navigate

Start with the Psychology Map to internalise the customer mindset. The Content Catalog is your creative brief — each row is a complete spec for a writer or designer. The Version Matrix shows the full production scope. The 30-Day Calendar sequences it. The Production Checklist tracks progress. Every table is sortable; click column headers to reorder.
